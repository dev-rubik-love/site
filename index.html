- How the algorithm works?
- We send posts that come to all users. Users rate these posts by “like” or “dislike”. We track these ratings and find people who have given the same rating to the same post. At first time the accuracy is low, but already after a week every user will have 2^28 combinations. It's making every user a really unique. With every new rate, a group matches narrows down literally to a one person, and the accuracy proportionally grows to almost 100%, It's like a Google algorithm but for people. Simple enough to be lightweight and scalable, efficient enough to be first in market

How does the algorithm solve the problem?
The algorithm gives us a key advantage for each item:

Accuracy of matching increases every day.

User 1 wrote "ping-pong" and user 2 wrote "table tennis" - it's the same, but match will be missed. With musical groups, such situations are especially.
